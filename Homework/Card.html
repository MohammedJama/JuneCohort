Khabib Nurmagomedov

Dagestani mixed martial artist

Khabib Nurmagomedov is a Dagestani former professional mixed martial artist who competed in the Lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship. In the UFC, he was the longest-reigning UFC Lightweight Champion ever, having held the title from April 2018 to March 2021.
